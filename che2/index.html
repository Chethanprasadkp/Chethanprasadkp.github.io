CMRIT ENGINEERING COLLEGE

ADDRESS

(132 Aecs Layout, Itpl Main Road, Kundalahalli, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560037)

CMRIT offers a wide variety of courses for Undergraduate and Postgraduate applicants in the fields of Engineering, Management, and Science, admission to which is both merit and entrance-based. Admission to CMRIT for the various academic programs is based on Karnataka CET/COMED-K/JEE for the undergraduate programs, admissions to postgraduate programs are based on performances in PGCET/GATE/CMAT/CAT. CMRIT Bangalore Cut-off for COMED-K, PGCET, and KCET varies every year.

ABOUT

CMR Institute of Technology, Bangalore was established in the year 200. The institute is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University and is approved by All India Council for Technical Education New Delhi. CMR Institute of Technology is recognised by Government of Karnataka. The institute offers Bachelors in Engineering, Masters in Engineering, Masters in Business Administration and Doctoral programmes to the students.
